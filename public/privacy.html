Your privacy is our priority. Learn how Speed Lens protects your data.

Last updated: July 31, 2025

Data Collection

Speed Lens is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data to provide you with accurate speed testing services:

  • Speed Test Results: Download/upload speeds, ping, and jitter measurements
  • Basic Network Info: IP address (for server selection), connection type, and ISP information
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type (for optimization purposes)
  • Usage Statistics: Number of tests performed (stored locally)

Data Storage

Your privacy is protected through our data storage practices:

  • Local Storage: Test history and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No Account Required: We don't require registration or personal information
  • Temporary Data: Server communication data is processed temporarily and not stored
  • No Tracking: We don't use tracking cookies or analytics that identify you personally

Data Usage

We use collected data solely for:

  • Providing accurate speed test results
  • Selecting the best test servers for your location
  • Improving the accuracy and performance of our service
  • Displaying your test history and analytics (stored locally)

Data Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not share your personal data:

  • No Data Sales: We never sell your data to third parties
  • No Advertising: We don't share data with advertising networks
  • Anonymous Analytics: Any analytics are aggregated and anonymized
  • Legal Compliance: Data may only be shared if required by law

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Clear History: Delete your test history anytime using the clear history button
  • No Persistent Tracking: Close your browser to end the session
  • Offline Capable: The app works offline for previously loaded resources
  • Data Export: Export your test data in various formats

Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • No sensitive data transmission beyond test requirements
  • Local storage encryption where supported
  • Regular security updates and improvements
